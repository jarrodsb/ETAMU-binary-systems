## Results

- Convolutional neural networks sample just the area around a given neuron. Can be repeated across the entire image. More efficient than dense networks.
- Autoencoders involve *unsupervised* learning and use compression to force the neural network to learn the most important features of a dataset, and reproduce an input based on those features. This is the beginning of the concept of generative techniques.
- PCA is another way to find the most important features of a dataset, and can be used for pre-training a neural network so that it doesn't require taking in a very large parameter space.
